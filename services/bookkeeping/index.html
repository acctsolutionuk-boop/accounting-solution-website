Frequently Asked Questions

What is bookkeeping and why does my business need it?

Bookkeeping is the systematic recording of all business income and expenditure. It is the foundation for tax returns, understanding profit, and managing cash flow. HMRC requires businesses to keep adequate records.

Do you offer Xero and QuickBooks bookkeeping in Brighton?

Yes. We are experienced with both platforms and can set up, migrate, or manage your books on either. We also provide training so you can handle day-to-day entries yourself if preferred.
