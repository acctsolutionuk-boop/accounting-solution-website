    Common questions

    Are these prices fixed, or will I get a final quote?

    These are the typical prices for clients in each category — not a low introductory rate. They’re shown as “from” figures because every business is a little different, so we’ll confirm your exact monthly fee at a free, no-obligation consultation — with no surprises once you’re a client.

    Do I need to use specific software?

    No. We provide our own MTD-compliant software as standard, included in every package. If you already use Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent or similar, we’re happy to work with whatever you’ve got.

    What is MTD for Income Tax and do I need it?

    Making Tax Digital for Income Tax became mandatory from April 2026 for self-employed people and landlords earning over £50,000/year (dropping to £30,000 from April 2027). If that’s you, it means quarterly digital submissions to HMRC instead of one annual return — we handle the whole process. It doesn’t apply to limited companies or to standalone personal tax returns.

    I’m switching from another accountant — is that complicated?

    Not at all. We contact your previous accountant, transfer all your records, and there’s no gap in your accounting. Usually complete within two weeks.
